The Best Movies According to Benjamin

My top 3 movies of my adolescence.

The Butterfly effect

A movie which simply explains the consequences of time traveling in a way that involves dramatically, suspensefully and ethically the viewer. The one with Asthon Kutcher is the right, the other ones are boring.

Hannibal

This suspense movie involves you in the way the vilain is thinking and put you in situations where you are constantly quesitoning your moral principles. An exceptional vilain who deserves a detailed examination.

Melody Fair

This one is a very cliche romantic comedy of some boys turning to adolescents with a beatiful Bee Gees soundtrack. Mostly I found it entertained because I lack the type of adventures they show when I was their ages.
